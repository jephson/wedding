Accommodation

  • The Angel Hotel is near to the park
  • The Travelodge on the Parade is also very close and somewhat cheaper

Restaurants

  • A firm favourite of ours is Kayal a fantastic South Indian cuisine that copes very well with vegetarian and gluten-free requirements.
  • Two nice pubs that serve food are The Regency Arms and The Royal Pug.
  • The Thai Elephant is also gorgeous in both decor and food.
